ABOUT US


M.R.S (MACHINE REBUILDING & SALES) IS AN INTERNATIONAL MACHINE TOOL COMPANY, DEVELOPED TO SERVE INDUSTRIES INVOLVED IN METAL CUTTING PROCESSES.

ESTABLISHED IN 1990, WE NOW HAVE GROWN INTO A GLOBAL MACHINE TOOL COMPANY WITH BRANCH OFFICES LOCATED IN SE ASIA AND THE MIDDLE EAST.

OUR PROFESSIONAL STAFF ARE EXPERIENCED IN MACHINING APPLICATIONS, CNC OPERATIONS/PROGRAMMING, REPAIR & MAINTENANCE SERVICES.

WE CONSTANTLY STRIVE TO PROVIDE THE BEST POSSIBLE SERVICES BY MAINTAINING CLOSE RELATIONSHIPS WITH BOTH OUR CUSTOMERS AND SUPPLIERS.

OUR SERVICES


MACHINE REBUILDING & SALES OFFERS IN-HOUSE FACILITIES TO CARRY OUT MINOR OR COMPLETE REBUILD/RECONDITIONING OF YOUR MACHINE TOOLS OR MAJOR COMPONENTS.

WE ALSO CAN PROVIDE FULL CNC SYSTEM RETROFITS (USING FANUC OR MITSUBISHI SYSTEMS) COMPLETE WITH NEW MOTORS & DRIVES.

  • BEDWAY GRINDING AND SCRAPING
  • BALLSCREW RECONDITIONING
  • HEADSTOCK RECONDITIONING (GEAR REPLACEMENT, SPINDLE BEARINGS, LUBRICATION SYSTEMS)
  • TURRET RECONDITIONING
  • OVERHAUL LUBRICATION SYSTEMS (PUMPS, FILTERS, LINES, METERING VALVES)
  • POWER CHUCK SERVICING
  • NEW OPERATOR PANELS (BUTTONS, SWITCHES, WIRING, ETCHING OF FACE PANEL LABELS)
  • PAINTING/FINISHING
  • RETROFIT NEW CNC SYSTEMS (FANUC/MITSUBISHI)

OUR PARTNERS


WE ARE PARTNERED WITH SEVERAL MACHINE TOOL AND ACCESSORY MANUFACTURERS FOR AUTHORISED SERVICE AND SUPPORT TO OUR CUSTOMERS.
